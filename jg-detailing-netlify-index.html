Everything you need to know before booking your detail.

How long does a detail take?

It depends on the service. Silver Interior takes ~1.5 hours, Gold or Platinum runs 4‚Äì5 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating can take 8+ hours. We'll always give you a clear time estimate at booking.

Do you come to me?

Pick-up and drop-off is available! We prefer to work at our location for the best results, but we're happy to coordinate for your convenience.

What if it rains?

We work rain or shine. Weather will never cancel your appointment ‚Äî we're committed to delivering excellent results regardless of conditions.

Yes! Excessive pet hair may incur a small upcharge ‚Äî just mention it when booking and we'll give you an accurate quote.

How should I prepare my vehicle?

Please remove all personal belongings before your appointment. Center console must be emptied if you want it cleaned.

We accept cash, card, and Venmo. A 25% non-refundable deposit is required at booking, applied toward your final balance on the day of service.
